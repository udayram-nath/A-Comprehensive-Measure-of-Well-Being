# Use Case Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Predicting Very High HDI
High life expectancy, strong schooling years, high GNI → Very High HDI (≥ 0.800)
Example: Norway (HDI: 0.966)

## Scenario 2: Identifying Development Gaps
Mid-range values → Medium HDI (0.550–0.699)
Example: India (HDI: 0.644)

## Scenario 3: Assessing Low Development
Low life expectancy, limited schooling, low GNI → Low HDI (< 0.550)
Example: Ethiopia (HDI: 0.492)
