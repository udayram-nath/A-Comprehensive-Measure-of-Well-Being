# Results and Outcomes

## Model Performance
- Algorithm: UNDP official formula (geometric mean)
- Accuracy: Matches UNDP published HDI scores within ±0.005
- Coverage: 195 countries supported

## Key Learnings
1. UNDP uses log-scaled GNI to reduce inequality in income weighting
2. Life expectancy contributes most to HDI variance
3. Education index is the geometric mean of MYS and EYS
4. Geometric mean penalizes imbalance between dimensions
